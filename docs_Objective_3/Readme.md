## Expected Outcome

The **WaterWise Intelligent Decision Support Services (DSS)** module will provide comprehensive analytics, AI-powered demand forecasting, anomaly detection, and intelligent recommendations that enable barangay officials to make informed decisions for water distribution, consumption monitoring, leak detection, and long-term resource planning. The integrated dashboard will present real-time insights, predictive analytics, and actionable recommendations to support efficient and sustainable water management.
